Orange Almond Olive Oil Cake

Equipment

  • 8 or 9-inch springform pan

Ingredients

Cake:

  • 4 large eggs, separated
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 large orange, zest and juice (‚âà1/2 cup)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable or extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/8 tsp almond extract or orange extract (optional)
  • 3 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 tbsp baking powder
  • Sliced almonds
  • Powdered sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F and line or grease an 8‚Äì9 inch springform pan.
  2. In a large bowl, beat sugar and egg yolks until light and foamy.
  3. Add orange zest, juice, olive oil, and almond or orange extract (if using). Beat for 2 minutes.
  4. Fold in almond flour and baking powder until smooth and combined.
  5. In a separate bowl, beat egg whites until stiff peaks form, then gently fold into the batter.
  6. Pour batter into prepared pan, level, and sprinkle with sliced almonds.
  7. Bake 40 minutes or until golden and set. Test with a toothpick for doneness.
  8. Allow cake to cool, remove from pan, and serve with a dusting of powdered sugar.

Notes

  • For extra flavor, let the cake rest for a few hours to allow the orange zest to infuse the batter.
  • Make sure egg whites are beaten to stiff peaks for a light and airy texture.
