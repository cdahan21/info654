Red Velvet Cake

Equipment

  • 3 small loaf pans (or one large loaf pan)
  • Piping tip (optional)

Ingredients

Cake:

  • 2 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened 100% cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup buttermilk, room temperature
  • 2 1/2 tbsp red food coloring

Frosting:

  • 14 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 cups powdered sugar, sifted

Directions

Make the Cake:

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (180¬∞C). Grease and line loaf pans with parchment.
  2. Whisk flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl.
  3. Beat butter and sugar until smooth. Add eggs, one at a time.
  4. Mix in oil, vinegar, vanilla, buttermilk, and food coloring until combined.
  5. Add dry ingredients and mix just until smooth. Don‚Äôt overmix.
  6. Divide batter evenly between pans and bake 30‚Äì35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool completely.

Make the Frosting:

  1. Beat cream cheese, butter, and vanilla until creamy.
  2. Gradually mix in powdered sugar until fluffy.

Assemble the Cake:

  1. Slice cooled loaves into 3 layers (or use 3 separate loaves).
  2. Spread frosting between each layer.
  3. Frost the outside of the cake.
  4. Optional (but encouraged):
    1. Apply a thin crumb coat around the sides.
    2. Pipe swirls on top for a finished look.
