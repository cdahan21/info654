Earl Grey Black Sesame Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 6 tbsp black tahini or black sesame paste

Sugar Coating (optional):

Infuse the Butter:

  1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium-low heat.
  2. Add Earl Grey tea, cover, and steep on very low heat for 5 minutes without boiling.
  3. Remove from heat and cool until just warm.

Make the Dough:

  1. In a large bowl, mix the butter with both sugars until smooth.
  2. Whisk in egg and vanilla, then stir in baking soda and baking powder.
  3. Fold in flour until fully combined.

Add Black Sesame Swirl:

  1. Divide dough into two bowls and add ~3 tbsp black tahini or paste to each.
  2. Gently swirl with a knife, leaving pockets of paste for a marbled effect.

  1. Scoop 2 tbsp (‚âà1.6 oz) dough per cookie onto parchment-lined trays.
  2. Chill again, uncovered, for 1 hour.

Prepare Sugar Coating (Optional):

  1. Mix sugar and tea in a shallow bowl.
  2. Roll chilled dough balls in the sugar mixture before baking.

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).
  2. Bake cookies on the middle rack for 7 minutes, then firmly tap the tray on the counter.
  3. Bake 4‚Äì5 more minutes until edges are lightly browned and centers are just set.
  4. Cool a few minutes on tray, then transfer to a wire rack. Repeat with remaining cookies.

  • Optional coating: Rolling in sugar adds a delicate crisp and Earl Grey aroma.
