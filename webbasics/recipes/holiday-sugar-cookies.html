Holiday Sugar Cookies

Classic holiday sugar cookies with a simple, sweet icing.

Perfect for holiday decorating and gifting, these cookies are festive, flavorful, and fun to make.

Directions

Make the Cookie Dough:

  1. Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, beat butter and sugar on high until smooth, ~3 minutes.
  3. Add egg, vanilla, and almond extract (if using); beat until combined.
  4. Mix in dry ingredients on low until just combined. Dough may be soft; add 1 tbsp flour if sticky.

  1. Divide dough in half and roll each portion to 1/4-inch thickness between floured parchment sheets.

  2. Whisk in remaining 1/2 tbsp water if needed until icing holds a ribbon shape.
  3. Add gel coloring if desired, starting with 1‚Äì2 drops at a time.

Decorate:

  1. Use dipping, squeeze bottles, or piping bags to decorate cookies. Outline first, then fill in.
  2. Add sprinkles immediately if desired.

Set Icing and Store:

  1. Allow icing to dry for up to 24 hours.
  2. Store decorated cookies up to 5 days at room temperature or 10 days in the refrigerator.

Notes

  • Chilling the dough helps keep the cookies from spreading and makes cutting easier.
  • Almond extract is optional but adds a warm, festive flavor.
