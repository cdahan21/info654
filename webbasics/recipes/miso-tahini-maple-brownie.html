Miso Tahini Maple Brownies

Directions

Prepare the Swirl:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, miso paste, and maple syrup until smooth. Set aside.

Make the Brownies:

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (177¬∞C) and line a square baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium saucepan over low heat, melt butter with cocoa powder and sugar, stirring until combined. Do not burn. Set aside to cool slightly.
  3. Add vanilla to the mixture and stir to combine.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing until incorporated.
  5. Fold in flour and chocolate chunks until evenly distributed.
  6. Spread batter evenly in the prepared pan.
  7. Dollop the miso-tahini-maple mixture over the batter. Use a knife or spatula to swirl it into the brownies.
  8. Optional (but encouraged):
    1. Sprinkle sesame seeds if desired and remaining chocolate chunks.
  9. Bake for 25 minutes. Allow to cool to room temperature or overnight for extra fudginess.

Notes

  • These brownies taste even better the next day, as the flavors deepen and the texture becomes fudgier.
  • You can use different types of chocolate or cocoa depending on your preference.
